CI Defect Reduction — Where to Aim the Next Improvement Cycle

A question-first DMAIC analysis answering a single operational decision: given one improvement team and one cycle, where should a plant aim it? Screened sixteen candidate factors, rejected twelve as statistical noise, and isolated three controllable drivers — maintenance hours, quality score, and production volume. Flag rates climb monotonically with each adverse condition present (47% → 96%), pointing the countermeasure at equipment reliability rather than the maintenance calendar itself. Built on a synthetic dataset as a methodology demonstration.
